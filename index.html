Step-by-step store setup

Six steps to opening day

Follow this sequence exactly — each step builds on the previous. Do not go live before all 6 are complete.

01

Account & shop creation

  • Create Etsy seller account using the brand email
  • Shop name: ProjectMira — match Shopify + social handles
  • Set location: USA · Currency: USD
  • Connect bank account for payouts · Enable PayPal
  • Complete identity verification before listing
02

Shop branding & visual identity

  • Shop icon: PM submark logo, square crop, clean background
  • Banner: warm botanical flatlay — Lavender Dream + Rose + Royal Elixir
  • Color direction: cream #F7EDD8, earth brown, gold accents
  • Tagline: "Ancient Indian Wellness. Modern Skin Rituals."
  • All visuals must match pmprojectmira.com aesthetic
03

Shop story & about section

  • Paste the pre-written Shop Story into Etsy's "About" section
  • Highlight Mirabai heritage and Ayurvedic roots
  • Add 5 shop photos: flatlay, ingredients, pour shot, skin, packaging
  • Weave in SEO: natural body oil · ayurvedic skincare · handmade oil
  • Add video walkthrough of the handmade process if available
04

Policies, shipping & SEO settings

  • Write Refund Policy matching the Shopify site policy
  • Build free US shipping for orders $50+ into pricing
  • Processing time: 1–3 business days in shipping profile
  • Enable Etsy Offsite Ads from day 1
  • Set all 13 tags per listing — mix broad + niche keywords
05
Urgent — do before launch

Restock critical inventory

  • All 3 gift sets are sold out on Shopify — restock before listing on Etsy
  • Haldi Harmony scrub currently sold out — great bundle partner
  • Confirm 50ml + 100ml variants ready for all hero products
  • Royal Elixir in 100ml is high-demand for Oud shoppers on Etsy
06

Photography & mockup prep

  • Main photo: clean cream/white bg, product center-frame
  • Photo 2: lifestyle — oil poured onto skin, warm light
  • Photo 3: botanical flatlay — ingredients beside the bottle
  • Photo 4: size comparison (50ml vs 100ml side by side)
  • Photo 5: gift-ready packaging shot · Use Canva mockups for gaps
Listing strategy

Product launch sequence & SEO positioning

All singles go live before bundles — this lets Etsy's algorithm cross-reference your products and boosts overall shop visibility before the high-ticket sets appear.

Week 1 — Hero launches

Lavender Dream · Muscle Zen Harmony · Royal Elixir

Highest Etsy search demand. Lead with keyword-first titles. Muscle recovery oil and oud body oil are underserved niches — strong early-mover advantage.

lavender sleep oilmuscle recovery oiloud body oilcalming body oil
Week 2

Timeless Rose Elegance · Rejuvenating Nectar

Both highly giftable. Push sensitive skin angle on Timeless Rose. Anti-aging positioning for Rejuvenating Nectar using the Ashwagandha + Amla + Saffron + Bakuchi keywords.

rose body oilsensitive skin oilayurvedic anti-aging
Week 3

Positive Energy Burst · Sunshine Armor · Haldi Harmony

Sunshine Armor is a seasonal anchor — lead with "natural SPF body oil" and "pre-swim protection" for summer traffic. Haldi Harmony rounds out the collection as an upsell.

citrus energy oilnatural sun protectionpre-swim oil
Week 4–5 — Bundle launches

Ananda Collection · Zen Blossom · Vitality Trio · Build Your Own Set

Once all singles are indexed, launch the gift sets. These are the highest-ticket items ($85–$90) and become the primary revenue driver at holiday peaks.
